Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible for courses you must:

  1. Have an active health insurance policy
  2. Be over the age of 18 by the time the course begins

COVID Notice
All courses are subject to change based on CDC guidelines. If courses are cancelled based on COVID restrictions, a full refund will be provided. CDC guidelines will be followed throughout courses, and students may be asked to leave without refund if not adhering to policies. Students will be asked to complete a COVID Waiver to acknowledge policies prior to course start.

Cancellation & Refund Policy
Payment required in full at time of registration. After registration, refunds minus a $25 cancellation fee are available until 14 days prior to the start of the first course date. No refunds available after 14 days before first course date. If courses are cancelled based on COVID restrictions, a full refund will be provided. Courses that do not meet the minimum enrollment may be cancelled with full refund or offer transfers to other course dates. For the safety of all, students experiencing any possible symptoms of COVID or exposed to anyone with COVID symptoms should report them immediately. Students experiencing COVID symptoms will work with course coordinator to adjust plans or provide refund depending on situation.
